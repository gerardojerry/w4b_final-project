Santa Cruz Island

Located 32.6 miles from Ventura, California, Santa Cruz Island is the biggest of the five islands that make up the Channel Islands.

Famous for its unique ecology, Santa Cruz Island is home to hundreds of endemic species that can't be found on the mainland.

Santa Cruz Island is also home to the UCSB Natural Reserve System, The Nature Conservancy, and The National Parks Service.
